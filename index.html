FAQs

Good questions. Here are the answers.

Everything you need to know about caravan detailing near me — and how we work in Perth.

What exactly is caravan detailing?

Caravan detailing goes far beyond a standard wash. It involves a thorough, multi-step clean and protection process — removing road grime, black streaks, oxidation, and built-up residue from every surface, then applying protective products to keep your van looking great for longer. Think of it as a proper detail, not just a rinse-off.

How does the mobile service work?

Simple — you book, I come to you. My van is fully self-contained with its own water supply and power, so I don't need access to your hose or power points (though it helps if available). I can come to your home, your workplace, a caravan park, or a storage facility anywhere across Perth.

Which areas of Perth do you cover?

I service most of the Perth metro area — from Joondalup in the north down to Mandurah in the south, including the Hills, Eastern Suburbs, and coastal areas. If you're searching for mobile caravan detailing Perth, chances are we cover your suburb. Just get in touch and I'll confirm your area.

How does Perth's weather affect the detailing process?

Perth's intense UV and heat can cause real damage to your van's exterior — fading paint, cracking seals, and oxidising fibreglass are all common issues here. Our treatments specifically address UV damage, and the sealants and ceramic coatings we use are chosen for Perth conditions. We'd also recommend scheduling out of peak heat hours in summer — early morning slots fill up fast!

How long does a caravan detail take?

It depends on the size of your van and the package you choose. The Essential takes around 2–3 hours, The Signature is typically 4–5 hours, and The Showroom can take a full day (6–8 hours). Larger motorhomes or fifth-wheelers may take a little longer. I'll give you a realistic time estimate when you book.

Do I need to do anything to prepare my van?

Not much! Just make sure I have access to your van and there's enough room to work around it. Removing any loose items from the exterior (chairs, mats, etc.) is helpful. For interior packages, clearing out personal belongings from cupboards and the bench is appreciated — but I'll take care of everything else.

What products do you use on caravans?

I use professional-grade, caravan-specific products that are safe for fibreglass, aluminium, acrylic windows, rubber seals, and canvas. Everything is chosen to be effective without causing damage. I'll always use the right product for your van's specific surfaces — not a one-size-fits-all approach.

How often should I get my caravan detailed?

For regular travellers, we recommend a maintenance detail every 3–6 months to keep on top of build-up and protect the finish. If your van's been in storage or come back from a long trip, a full detail before and after the season is ideal. Perth's harsh sun means more frequent UV protection top-ups are worth it.
