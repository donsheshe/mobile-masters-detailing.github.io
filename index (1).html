GOT QUESTIONS?

Everything you need to know about mobile caravan detailing in Perth. Can't find your answer — just give us a call.

Caravan detailing is a thorough, multi-step clean and protection process that goes way beyond a standard wash. It removes road grime, black streaks, oxidation, and built-up residue, then applies protective products to keep your van looking great for longer. In Perth's harsh UV conditions, regular detailing protects your investment and extends the life of your van's surfaces.

Simple — you book, we come to you. Our van is fully self-contained with its own water supply and power, so we don't need access to your hose or power points (though it helps if available). We can come to your home, workplace, caravan park, or storage facility anywhere in Perth.

Perth's intense UV and heat causes real damage over time — fading gelcoat, cracking seals, and oxidising fibreglass are all common issues for WA caravanners. The sealants and ceramic coatings we use are specifically chosen for WA conditions. We recommend booking early-morning slots in summer to get the best results.

The Essential takes around 2–3 hours, The Signature is typically 4–5 hours, and The Showroom can take a full day (6–8 hours). Larger motorhomes or fifth-wheelers may take a little longer. We'll give you a realistic time estimate when you book — and we won't rush the job.

A wash removes surface dirt. A full detail goes much deeper — clay bar treatment, interior deep clean, UV protection, sealants, and targeted treatments for specific issues like black streaks or oxidation. A proper detail can add years to the life of your van's exterior and significantly improve its resale value.

For regular travellers, every 3–6 months is ideal. If your van has been in storage or just returned from a long trip, a full detail before and after the season is recommended. Perth's harsh sun means UV protection top-ups are worth doing more frequently than most people realise.

Absolutely. We detail all types of recreational vehicles — standard caravans, pop-tops, fifth-wheelers, motorhomes, camper trailers, and more. Larger vehicles may take longer and have adjusted pricing — just let us know what you have when you enquire and we'll give you an accurate quote.
